Fire Disaster Management System

1. Building Fire

Description: Fire in residential or commercial buildings.

Action: Evacuate immediately and call the fire department.

2. Forest Fire

Description: Uncontrolled fire in a forest or wildland area.

Action: Report to authorities and avoid the area.

3. Vehicle Fire

Description: Fire in cars, trucks, or other vehicles.

Action: Exit the vehicle and move to a safe distance.

4. Industrial Fire

Description: Fire in factories or industrial facilities.

Action: Follow evacuation protocols and alert emergency services.

5. Electrical Fire

Description: Fire caused by electrical faults or overloads.

Action: Turn off power and use a fire extinguisher if safe.

6. Chemical Fire

Description: Fire involving hazardous chemicals.

Action: Evacuate and avoid inhaling fumes.

7. Kitchen Fire

Description: Fire caused by cooking accidents.

Action: Use a fire extinguisher or cover with a lid to smother flames.
